CocoaHeads Belgium is a new initiative to bring Cocoa enthusiasts together. The first meeting is scheduled on September 28, 2009 at the IBBT offices.

This occasion seemed a good excuse to start a list with all Belgian iPhone and iPad applications, commercial or not, useful or utterly useless, French, Dutch or English. The application needs to be available in the App Store and designed or developed by a Belgian.
