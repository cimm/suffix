--- layout: post title: How many days in a month date: 2010-01-31 23:00:00 updated: 2022-05-06 09:00:00 description: Ruby one-liner to calculate how many days there are in a month. ---

The following returns the number of days in a given year and month, including leap years.

{% highlight ruby %} (Date.new(year, 12, 31) << (12-month)).day {% endhighlight %}

The beauty of Ruby.
